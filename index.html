Meet Your Teacher

Hi, I'm Miss Chaunte!

Welcome to Bright Stars Daycare! I'm Chaunte America, and I opened my in-home daycare because I believe every child deserves a place that feels just like home, full of warmth and giggles.

Before going in-home, I spent years working in a childcare center, and I bring that same hands-on experience to every day here. Our days are filled with circle time, story books, arts and crafts, and plenty of holiday fun. There is always something new to learn and lots of room to just be a kid.
